FAQS

How do I choose the right carpet for my space?

▼

Consider factors such as the room's size, traffic level, desired style, and your budget. You can also browse our website for different carpet options and use filters to narrow down your choices.

What are the different carpet materials available?

▼

We offer a variety of carpet materials, including wool, nylon, polyester, and polypropylene. Each material has its own characteristics, such as durability, softness, or stain resistance. Check the product descriptions for more details.

How do I measure my room for carpet installation?

▼

Measure the length and width of your room using a tape measure. Multiply these measurements to get the square footage. We recommend adding a little extra to account for any cutting or fitting during installation.

What is the recommended cleaning and maintenance for carpets?

▼

Regular vacuuming is essential to keep your carpet clean. For spills or stains, blot the area immediately with a clean cloth and use a carpet cleaner suitable for the specific stain. We also recommend professional deep cleaning periodically.

Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on your carpets?

▼

Yes, we offer warranties on select carpets. The warranty details, including coverage period and specific terms, can be found on the product page or in the product documentation.

What are your shipping and delivery options?

▼

We offer various shipping methods, including standard and expedited options. Shipping fees and estimated delivery times will be provided during the checkout process based on your location and the chosen shipping method.

How can I return or exchange a carpet if I'm not satisfied?

▼

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support within the specified return window. We will guide you through the return or exchange process, including any applicable fees or requirements.

Are there any discounts or promotions available?

▼

We occasionally offer discounts and promotions. To stay updated on our latest offers, sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media. You can also check our website's "Promotions" section for any ongoing deals.
